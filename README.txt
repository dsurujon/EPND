This program geenrates a plot of a dna walk on a coding (rdeA) and noncoding (fbxA) sequence. The nucleotide composition bias that's being plotted is described in detail in the following publication:

Yin, C. & Yau, S. S.-T. A Fourier characteristic of coding sequences: origins and a non-Fourier approximation. J. Comput. Biol. 12, 1153–1165 (2005).
